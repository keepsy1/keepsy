𝟐 𝐒𝐚𝐦𝐮𝐞𝐥 𝟐𝟐:𝟑𝟑 (𝐊𝐉𝐕)

From the starry night sky
It was unexplainable gloomy eye
You, my friend, have seen me
As no one will ever be

Even if we were apart from distance
Our bond remains for persistence
You showed me your undying care
With such loving hugs we share

To the bright shining daylight
I will be holding on to our plight
Whether in sunny or in blue
I am forever grateful to have you, loveyouuu.
